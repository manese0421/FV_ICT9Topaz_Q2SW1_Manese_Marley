Meet the characters from the 2018 anime Violet Evergarden.

Main Characters

Photo Name Introduction
Violet Evergarden Violet is a former child soldier who now works as an Auto Memory Doll, helping people put their emotions into letters. She hopes that by becoming an AMD (Auto Memory Doll), she will find out the true meaning of "I love you"-- the last words Major Gilbert said to her.
Major Gilbert Bougainvillea Gilbert was Violet's superior in the military and the first (and only) person who treated her with kindness. He gave her the name "Violet" to teach her that she is human, not a military toy and then disappears after the war.
Claudia Hodgins Hodgins is the president of the CH Postal Company and a former friend of Major Gilbert. He took in Violet after the war and gave her the job of Auto Memory Doll to help her make peace with the fact that she may never see Gilbert again.
Cattleya Baudelaire Cattleya is one of the most talented Auto Memory Dolls at CH Postal Company. She is elegant, confident, and often gives advice about human emotions that are unfamiliar to Violet.
Benedict Blue Benedict is a postman at the CH Postal Company and an old friend of Hodgins. He delivers letters across the land and shares a complicated yet friendly rivalry with Violet.
